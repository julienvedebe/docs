The following two date formats are supported: 

**YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ss** 

Where:

 YYYY	- four digits for year (2014).

 MM	- two digits for month (01 to 12, 01 = January).

 DD		- two digits for day of month (01 to 31).  

hh		- two digits for hours (00 to 23).  

mm		- two digits for minutes (00 to 59).  

ss		- two digits for seconds (00 to 59).

The date and time format for **Advanced Messaging**, **Live Position Latest**, **Vehicle Journey Latest**, and **Driver Journey Latest** includes milliseconds, therefore the date/time format is as follows:

**YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:**[**ss.ms**](http://ss.ms)** **

Where:

YYYY 	- four digits for year (2014).

MM 	- two digits for month (01 to 12, 01 = January).

DD 		- two digits for day of month (01 to 31).

hh 		- two digits for hours (00 to 23). 

mm 	- two digits for minutes (00 to 59).

ss 		- two digits for seconds (00 to 59). 

ms 		– three digits for milliseconds (000 to 999).

## **Coordinated Universal Time (UTC)**

All times returned and sent to the **Connect API** are in Coordinated Universal Time (UTC).
