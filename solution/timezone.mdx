---
title: "Timezones"
---

Dates and time are held in UTC and converted into the specified timezone when displaying details in the **myConnectedFleet portal**.

Allowed timezone that can be set are:

- Australia/Sydney
- Australia/Brisbane
- Australia/Adelaide
- Australia/Darwin
- Australia/Perth
- Atlantic/Azores
- Europe/Prague
- Europe/Berlin
- Europe/Copenhagen
- Europe/London

- Europe/Amsterdam
- Europe/Warsaw
- Europe/Lisbon
- Europe/Madrid
- Europe/Stockholm
- Africa/Luanda

- America/New_York
