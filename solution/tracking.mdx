**Tracking** 
my Connected Fleet can report on the the location of a resource in real time. In addition to the geographic location, **my Connected Fleet** captures direction of travel, addresses, time/date and other information items depending on the vehicle type (e.g. door open events) and country settings (e.g speed in France).  


**Tracing** 
Recorded events are also avaialble histroically (tracing), as **Journeys**, explained in the following section.
