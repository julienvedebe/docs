---
title: "Tracking"
---

**myConnectedFleet** can report on the the location of a resource in real time. 

In addition to the geographic location, **myConnectedFleet** captures direction of travel, addresses, time/date and other information items depending on the vehicle type (e.g. door open events) and country settings (e.g speed in France).  


These recorded events are also avaialble histroically), as **Journeys**, explained in the following section.
