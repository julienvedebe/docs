---
title: 'Vehicles'
---

A vehicle is a tracked resource that represents any asset equipped with an On-Board Unit (OBU). Vehicles can include cars, vans, trucks, trailers, tractors, and other motorised or towed assets.

- A vehicle can be assigned to a group for organisational or reporting purposes.
Note: A vehicle’s group assignment does not need to match the group assigned to a driver.

- When a vehicle is active in the customer’s fleet (status: IN-CIRCULATION), its latest position is returned in the Live Position API response.
